Creativity in Early Childhood Curriculum

Creativity is a fundamental aspect of early childhood education, shaping children's ability to think critically, solve problems, and express themselves...

Art

Encouraging self-expression through visual arts, painting, drawing, and sculpture.

Drama and Puppetry

Supporting storytelling, role-playing, and emotional expression.

Movement and Music

Developing physical coordination and imaginative play through dance and action-based activities.

Language and Literacy

Fostering storytelling, creative writing, and oral communication.

Science

Encouraging curiosity, experimentation, and inquiry-based learning.

Engineering

Exploring construction, design thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Technologies

Integrating digital tools, coding, and multimedia storytelling.

Mathematics and Numeracy

Encouraging problem-solving, pattern recognition, and spatial awareness through hands-on activities.
